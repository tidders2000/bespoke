OWNERSHIP +

When purchasing a yacht it is important to consider the use of corporate structures which can not only assist with tax and VAT planning but also ring fence exposure and limit liability.

A full list of corporate services can be found on our Corporate Services page which we provide alongside other yacht management services.

Fiducs can also assist in the arrangement of appropriate insurance cover and purchase finance through one of our established connections. For more information, please contact us.

Safety +

We provide ISM & ISPS services as a bespoke standalone service or as part of our full management package as well as flag state approved systems for vessels from PYC (Passenger Yacht Code) Vessels to yachts under 24 meters and 24/7 crisis support and full document control.

CREW EMPLOYMENT & PAYROLL +

Fiducs offers crew employment and payroll including flag state approved MLC seafarers agreements (SEA), maintaining crew records, monitoring crew certificates ensuring compliance with regulations and crew recruitment including vetting, back ground checks, referencing, security checks and drug testing.

Our concierge arm can also organise flight and travel arrangements for crew members.

OPERATIONS & LOGISTICS +

Fiducs Yacht Management will make arrangements to facilitate and safeguard the world-wide operation of the yacht such as assisting in berthing arrangements, bunkering and ship agents where required.

PROCURATION +

Our Concierge services can assist in a wide range of procuration services for yachts from travel arrangements to luxury personal shopping to parts and living incidentals to “boys toys”.
