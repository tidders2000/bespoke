Corporate Services

COMPANIES ARE THE MOST VERSATILE INTERFACE WITH THE WORLD

Companies are by far the best way to define individual interests, ring-fence exposure, limit liability and optimise tax mitigation. They are also the most versatile interface with the world at large, and the usual route for trading activities.

BSpoke 360 Trust and Corporate Services offering for companies includes:

  • Company formation and ongoing administration including bank and payments management
  • Structure planning (help with choosing the appropriate jurisdiction and the format for specific circumstances objectives)
  • Obligations fulfilment (taxation, statutory, formal recordings and filing, secretarial and registered office) company officers (directors, secretaries, registered agents and other representative persons)
  • Convening and holding of board meetings and preparation of minutes
  • Liaising with all stakeholders including shareholders,bankers,managers,accountants,auditors,taxation authorities
  • Back-office solutions
  • Book-keeping and accounting
Trading companies +

As well as operating requirements, the way a company is structured may need to take account of taxation benefits, VAT/sales taxes, import duties, local regulations and licence. BSpoke 360 help you build on a firm foundation from the start.

Based in the Isle of Man, BSpoke 360 operates in many jurisdictions around the world, providing corporate facilities for its clients, together with administration and commercial support.

Limited exposure and ring-fencing +

Limiting liability through incorporation, can play an important role in protecting your personal interests as a whole, and is ideal for ring-fencing specific exposures of particular assets or activities.

Asset holding +

Physical assets, such as property, commercial plants, vehicles, yachts and aircraft are accompanied by third party liability for loss or injury. Ownership of these assets through a company, provides a back-stop position to limit these exposures.

Structures +

Companies, either alone or in conjunction with trusts or foundations, are the cornerstones of taxation planning. This applies to both life-time planning and succession taxation planning.

Partnerships +

A partnership involves two or more interested parties. There are two types of partnerships: ‘general’ and ‘limited’. BSpoke 360 can help with all services related to partnerships, including registration, incorporation, statutory maintenance, administration and corporate services.

Registered Office +

If you are a licensed, corporate service provider in a jurisdiction other than the Isle of Man, BSpoke 360 can offer you a number of services, including:

  • company incorporation
  • obtaining registered office
  • maintenance of statutory records and filing
  • statutory return filing
General +

BSpoke 360 can form and administer companies in the Isle of Man and in other jurisdictions, as well as different types of entities, such as limited companies, partnerships, LLCs, international businesses, and more.

The Isle of Man benefits from 0% Corporation Tax for those companies held by non-residents. Whilst the Island may be separate from the UK, it applies VAT at the same level as the UK, which is administered by the Isle of Man Customs and Excise.
