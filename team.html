Meet the Team

JOHN BELL BSc (Hons) Computer Science, FCA

John founded what has become BSpoke360 in 1994 to complement his cross-border tax services. His career bias has been towards broad-based commercial consultancy including opportunity appraisal and planning, project management, corporate transactions and investment assessment and services. His approach has been driven by working closely with clients to best assess, interpret and address their specific requirements, a culture which continues to underpin BSpoke360 to day. Interests outside work include sailing, skiing, and as a writer of novels.

CHRIS STEWART

Chris joined the BSpoke360 family in June 2021. With a background in the Corporate Services world, starting in 1995, he has a wide understanding of the regulatory and corporate governance obligations of this sector. Finding a niche in the yachting world, her has gained vast experience in in this specific area dealing with such matters as registration, VAT compliance, ISM/ISPS and technical management. Married, with two extremely active boys, Chris finds his free time mostly providing “dad taxi” services; spending most weekends on the side-lines of hockey and football pitches around the Island. ANNA TEARE

With over 20 years compliance experience within the IOM Financial Services industry, Anna has been a member of the BSpoke360 team since March 2017 and was appointed to the Board in April 2018. A mum of 4 boys, evenings and weekends are often spent at the side of a football or rugby pitch or down at the athletics track. Anna is also a qualified coach with UK Athletics.

GRAHAM MOORE

Graham Is a Chartered Accountant with many years post qualification experience across a wide cross section of companies and sectors including as Principal of his own Chartered Accountancy practice. He joined BSpoke360 in May 2021 as a Director of the licensed entity. Married with one son, a granddaughter and twin grandsons, when not on Grandfather duty Graham is a motorsports enthusiast and participates in karting as chief mechanic for his son.

LIZ
