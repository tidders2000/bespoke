BUILD MANAGEMENT

A SUCCESSFUL BUILD PROJECT DELIVERS THE CONCEPTS AND DESIRES OF THE OWNER

BSpoke 360 provide yacht owners, captains and brokers with professional and impartial consultancy, as well as project management for new-build and re-fit projects, worldwide.

We approach construction management by putting ourselves in your shoes. What would concern us, if we were you? This drives our attention to detail, ensuring the protection of your investment, and achieving value and quality.

A successful build project delivers the concepts and desires of the owner. It should be an enjoyable experience, during which you, the owner maintains control, whilst being relieved of the detail and issues arising during a complex process. Making the right choices from the outset – from the design team, boatyard and builder to the support services, lawyers and insurers – is key to the project’s success.

Throughout the build project, planning and follow-through, we ensure potential issues are recognised and addressed quickly and efficiently. We have the experience, technical, financial and administrative experience, to deliver a great experience from beginning to end.

The primary objectives of our construction management are to ensure that:

  • the owner’s concepts and specifications are interpreted in the design and execution
  • the requirements for completion of a project have been comprehensively planned and responsibilities allocated
  • well-defined responsibilities are under-pinned by executed documentation, and understood by all parties involved
  • performance against contract is adhered to by all parties
  • all variations to the contract are on an agreed basis, and documented
  • payments are properly made, in accordance with the contract
  • finances (and other aspects of the project) are properly recorded and reported
  • obligations by law, regulation or otherwise (such as may impede the ultimate signing off of the vessel or the progress of construction) are addressed in a timely manner.
